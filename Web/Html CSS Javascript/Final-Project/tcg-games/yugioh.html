Yugioh

Yugioh is my first and most played card game. Yugioh is played using a deck of 40-60 cards. My personal preference is no more than 45 cards. Anything greater will slow down your deck. The execption to this rule would be if your deck is more control based and does not mind being slow. You and your opponent start at 8000 health. Using your deck that consists of monster, spell, and trap cards, you win by reducing your opponets life points to zero.
